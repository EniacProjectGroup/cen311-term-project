Who We Are?

Established in 2025, MS Cast management company is an agency that builds strong connections between artists, influencer, and brands, drawing on years of experience.

Our Vision

To be a leading management company in the drama and cinema industry with our innovative and actor-focused approach. To elevate industry standards by representing nationally and internationally recognized talents, and to contribute to the global success actors. By rapidly adapting to the changing dynamics of the digitalizing world, we aim to carry our actors' careers to sustainable success and to be a long-lasting, reliable business partner in the industry..

Our Mission

●To discover talented actors, unlock their potential, and manage their careers with a strategic approach.

●To establish transparent and strong relationships with producers, directors, and other stakeholders in the industry, creating the most suitable projects for our actors..

●To earn the trust of our actors and develop long-term collaborations with an ethical, honest, and professional service approach.

●To continuously learn and evolve, keeping up with the innovations in the industry to offer our actors the most up-to-date and effective strategies..
